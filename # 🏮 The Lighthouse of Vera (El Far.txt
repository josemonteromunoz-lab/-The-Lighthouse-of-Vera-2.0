# 🏮 The Lighthouse of Vera (El Faro de Vera) v3.0

**The Lighthouse of Vera** is an advanced, 360° adaptive AI support system designed for individuals with Down Syndrome and their entire support network.

## ✍️ Authorship & Vision
This project is an original creation by **José Montero Muñoz** and the **Navegante Omega Group**. 

We believe in the "Architecture of Human Systems" — shifting from a clinical-only perspective to an engineering and neurobiological approach that empowers the individual.

## 🛠️ Key Features
* **Dynamic Identity Mapping:** Mandatory handshake protocol to identify the user.
* **Adaptive UI:** Tailors complexity based on the profile (Friend, Parent, Teacher, or Doctor).
* **Multilingual & Multicultural:** Real-time adaptation to global languages and local norms.
* **Clean Interface:** Minimalist output, hiding all internal complex reasoning.

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Copy the content of `system_instruction.md`.
2. Paste it into your AI model's "System Instructions".
3. Start the conversation by saying "Hello".
